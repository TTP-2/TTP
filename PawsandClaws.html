Paws and claws

Paws and Claws is a trusted pet-sitting club dedicated to providing loving care for pets when their owners are away. Whether it’s a weekend trip or a long vacation, members can rely on experienced and passionate pet sitters to ensure their furry, feathered, or scaly friends receive the attention they deserve. The club connects pet owners with reliable caregivers who offer services like feeding, walking, playtime, and even overnight stays. Paws and Claws also fosters a supportive community where members can share pet care tips, attend workshops, and build lasting friendships with fellow animal lovers. With a focus on safety, reliability, and compassion, Paws and Claws makes pet sitting stress-free for both pets and their owners!
